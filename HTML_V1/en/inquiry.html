What speeds up a reply

Six things — we don't need more

  • Machine manufacturer and type — the same brand has dozens of models with different parts.
  • Year of manufacture (or serial number) — bearings and belts change between years.
  • Photo of the part — sharp, so the type label or engraving is legible.
  • Photo of the nameplate or marking directly on the part — the fastest way to identify it.
  • Drawing from the schematic, if you have one — a page from the manual or catalogue.
  • Control system — decisive for servo drives, sensors and encoders.
