2. What data we process and for what purpose

2.1 Inquiry form and inquiry communication

When you send us an inquiry via the website form or by e-mail, phone or WhatsApp, we process the data you provide to us. This typically includes:

  • identification and contact details — full name, company name, e-mail, phone;
  • machine and requested part details — manufacturer, type/model, year of manufacture, serial number, control system, part designation, description of the problem;
  • attachments — photos of the part, photos of the nameplate, drawings, manuals or videos you attach to the inquiry;
  • the content of further communication — e-mails, messages and notes created while handling your inquiry and any delivery.

We use this data to prepare a quote for you (price and delivery date), answer your questions, arrange delivery of the part and conduct related business communication with you.

Completing the required form fields is necessary for us to process the inquiry — without them we cannot prepare a quote. Optional details and attachments help us reply faster and more accurately, but providing them is voluntary.

2.2 Contract performance and legal obligations

If an inquiry leads to an order and delivery of a part, we process the data necessary to conclude and perform the contract, as well as data we are required to keep under legal regulations (in particular accounting and tax documents).

2.3 Website operation

The website is static and does not collect analytics data by itself. The hosting provider (see section 4) may briefly record data in the server's technical logs, such as the IP address, the date and time of the request, the browser type and the page you came from — to ensure the operation, security and stability of the website.

Handling the inquiry and related communication Taking steps prior to entering into a contract at your request, or our legitimate interest in handling your query (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f) GDPR) For the duration of handling the inquiry and then typically 24 months from the last communication; after that we delete the data unless a reason to keep it longer arises (e.g. a concluded order, a legal claim)

The consent to the processing of personal data that you grant in the inquiry form covers the processing of data and attachments for the purpose of handling the inquiry as described above. You may withdraw your consent at any time by e-mail at info@cnc-nahradnidily.cz; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out beforehand, nor processing necessary for the performance of a contract or compliance with a legal obligation.

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data and do not pass it on for third-party marketing purposes. The following may access the data to the extent necessary:

  • the website hosting provider — Netlify, Inc. (USA), which provides the operation and delivery of the pages for us, and the receipt of messages from the web form;
  • the e-mail mailbox provider, and possibly other services for receiving and processing inquiries;
  • part suppliers and manufacturers — where necessary to verify the availability, price and delivery of a specific part (typically limited to the part designation and machine details, and any necessary contact and delivery data);
  • carriers — to the extent necessary to deliver the shipment;
  • accounting, tax and legal advisors, where needed;
  • public authorities, where required by law.

Some technical service providers — in particular hosting (Netlify, Inc., USA) and possibly the e-mail provider — may process data on servers located outside the European Economic Area. In such cases the transfer is carried out in accordance with the GDPR, in particular on the basis of standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission, or an adequacy decision.

5. Your rights

In relation to the personal data we process about you, you have the following rights:

  • the right of access — to know whether and what data we process about you, and to obtain a copy of it;
  • the right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • the right to erasure ("the right to be forgotten") — in the cases laid down by law;
  • the right to restriction of processing;
  • the right to object to processing based on legitimate interest;
  • the right to data portability for the data you have provided to us, where processing is based on consent or a contract and is carried out by automated means;
  • the right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time;
  • the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

You can exercise your rights by e-mail at info@cnc-nahradnidily.cz or by phone at +420 702 805 614. To handle your request, we may ask you to verify your identity. We will deal with the request without undue delay, at the latest within one month.

The supervisory authority in the Czech Republic is the Office for Personal Data Protection (Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů), Pplk. Sochora 27, 170 00 Prague 7, www.uoou.cz.
