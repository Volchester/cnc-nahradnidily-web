About us

We're CNC machine service technicians.

Alongside servicing, we source spare parts for machines we know well — because we repair, assemble and commission them every day.

What we do

Service and parts from one source

Service and parts supply go hand in hand here. Thanks to our daily work on machines, we know how to deal with suppliers, what to ask and where the price hides overhead you don't have to pay.

We're not an authorised service centre or a big middleman — and that's our competitive edge. The same part an authorised dealer would charge you for with a markup covering all of its infrastructure, we get from the same supplier for a fraction.

Our rules

01

No guesses, only verified information

Before we order anything, we're sure it fits. If we're not sure, we say so up front.

02

Realistic delivery dates
